Overview

Purpose

Our website exists to promote our whitewater rafting business by showcasing our exciting guided trips, providing essential safety and booking information, and helping potential customers learn about the unforgettable adventure experiences we offer. Our goal is to build revenue through online bookings while sharing our passion for outdoor recreation and river conservation.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure-seeking individuals and groups, ranging from young adults (ages 18–35) looking for an adrenaline rush, to families and corporate teams interested in outdoor bonding experiences. These customers typically have moderate to high disposable income and are interested in outdoor recreation, fitness, and travel. Many are planning vacations or weekend getaways and want unique, memorable activities. Their needs include clear information on safety, trip options, pricing, and booking availability—needs that are often not fully met by other regional adventure sites. Most of our visitors will access the site via mobile devices while traveling or planning on-the-go, so our site will be fully mobile-responsive to support quick navigation and booking.
